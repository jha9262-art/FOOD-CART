Who We Are

The Community-Based Sustainable Food Network (CBSFN) is a collaborative initiative aimed at building a sustainable, local food system. We bring together farmers, food processors, distributors, and consumers to create a more resilient and environmentally friendly food network.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote sustainable food practices, support local businesses, and ensure that every community member has access to fresh, healthy food.

Our Core Principles

  • Renewability: Using resources sustainably and regenerating ecosystems.
  • Resilience: Creating food systems that can withstand environmental and economic challenges.
  • Equity: Ensuring fair access to food and opportunities for all.
  • Inclusion: Engaging community members in the food system.

What We Do

Our activities include:

  • Supporting local farmers through direct sales and partnerships.
  • Reducing food waste through composting and donation programs.
  • Encouraging community gardening and urban farming initiatives.
  • Educating the public on sustainable food practices.
